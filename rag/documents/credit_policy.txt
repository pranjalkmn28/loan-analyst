Internal Credit Policy — Retail Lending Division
Version 3.2 | Effective April 2024

PART A — RISK CLASSIFICATION FRAMEWORK

A.1 RISK SCORING MODEL
Risk Score 0-30 (Low Risk):
- Credit score 750+
- DTI below 30%
- Employment stability 5+ years
- No adverse credit history
- Decision: APPROVE with standard terms

Risk Score 31-55 (Medium Risk):
- Credit score 680-749
- DTI 30-45%
- Employment 2-5 years
- Minor credit blemishes acceptable
- Decision: CONDITIONAL_APPROVE — may require co-applicant or higher down payment

Risk Score 56-75 (High Risk):
- Credit score 650-679
- DTI 45-50%
- Employment less than 2 years or recent change
- Some adverse history
- Decision: REFER_TO_UNDERWRITER for manual review

Risk Score 76-100 (Critical Risk):
- Credit score below 650
- DTI above 50%
- Unstable employment
- Adverse credit history or fraud indicators
- Decision: REJECT

PART B — SPECIFIC PRODUCT RULES (HOME LOANS)

B.1 Minimum down payment: 20% of property value.
B.2 Maximum tenure: 30 years or until age 75, whichever is earlier.
B.3 Prepayment penalty: None for floating rate loans per RBI directive.
B.4 Co-applicant requirement: Mandatory if primary applicant DTI exceeds 40%.
B.5 Insurance: Home loan protection plan mandatory for loans above ₹30 lakhs.

PART C — FRAUD INDICATORS AND ESCALATION

C.1 AUTOMATIC REJECTION TRIGGERS:
- Forged documents (verified)
- Identity mismatch across documents
- Blacklisted employer or address
- Previous loan fraud record

C.2 MANUAL REVIEW TRIGGERS:
- Salary structure change in last 6 months exceeding 50%
- Multiple applications across lenders in 90-day window
- Business address same as residential address (self-employed)
- Round number salary (e.g. exactly ₹50,000, ₹1,00,000) — possible fabrication

C.3 INCOME INCONSISTENCY FLAGS:
- Bank credits lower than declared salary by more than 20%
- Irregular salary credit dates (varies by more than 5 days)
- Salary credits from unknown or shell entities
- Cash salary claims without supporting documentation

PART D — DECISION AUTHORITY MATRIX

D.1 Loan amount up to ₹25 lakhs: Branch credit officer authority.
D.2 Loan amount ₹25-75 lakhs: Regional credit manager authority.
D.3 Loan amount above ₹75 lakhs: Credit committee approval required.
D.4 All CONDITIONAL_APPROVE decisions: Minimum 2 officer sign-off required.
D.5 All REFER_TO_UNDERWRITER decisions: 5-day SLA for underwriter response.
